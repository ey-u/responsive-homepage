ABOUT ME

Hey there, my name's Aang, and I'm the avatar! I'm a fun-loving, adventurous air nomad with a great sense of humor. In my time off, you'll probably find me riding otter penguins or giant elephant koi with my friends! Fun fact: I'm one of the few Avatars to ever learn energybending!

THE "GAANG"

Katara

Katara's my waterbending teacher (and the love of my life)! Independent, strong-willed, and compassionate, she's the glue that keeps the team together. She'll never turn her back on people who need her.

Sokka

Sokka, the meat and sarcasm guy. Also, the strategist guy. His humor and his need to stick to a strict schedule helped us out a lot during our travels.

Toph

Toph is the greatest earthbender in the world. Don't believe me? Try fighting her. Not anyone can just invent metalbending in a pinch.

Zuko

Zuko's come a long way from chasing me and my friends around the world. From a banished prince to an irreplaceable friend, Zuko was instrumental in training me to defeat Firelord Ozai.

Suki

Suki, the leader of the Kyoshi Warriors, is a strong and dependable friend who has helped us in countless ways since we met. She protected Appa, helped Zuko and Sokka break out of prison, and helped us fight the Fire Nation. What can't she do?

Appa

Appa's my flying bison and my best friend! We've been together since he was a young calf, and we have a very strong bond. I guess that's what happens when you're stuck in ice together for 100 years.

Momo

Momo's my pet lemur! We found him after I was freed from my hundred-year slumber. Momo, Appa, and I are all that's left of the Air Nomad civilization.
